Surah An-Nas

Arabic Text:

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ النَّاسِ

Meaning:

"Say, I seek refuge in the Lord of mankind"

Key Points:

  • Last surah in the Quran
  • Protection from evil
  • Memorization tip: Repeat 3x before sleep

20 Sifat Wajib Allah

First 5 Sifat:

  1. Wujud (Allah exists)
  2. Qidam (Allah is eternal)
  3. Baqa' (Allah is everlasting)
  4. Mukhalafatuhu lil hawadith (Allah is different from creations)
  5. Qiyamuhu binafsih (Allah is self-sufficient)

Tip: Remember with acronym "WUJUD"
