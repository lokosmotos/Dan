1. Senses and Living Things

Five Senses:

  • 👀 Sight - Eyes
  • 👂 Hearing - Ears
  • 👃 Smell - Nose
  • 👅 Taste - Tongue
  • ✋ Touch - Skin

Living vs Non-living:

Living things can grow, breathe, reproduce, and need food/water.
